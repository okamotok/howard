Classrooms

AV Controls

The AV System is controlled by a wall mounted touchscreen. In rooms 527 and 529 the screen is located next to the door. In rooms 451, 452 and 453 it is located on the wall next to the whiteboard

Using a laptop with the classroom projector

The classroom Lecterns have cables to connect a laptop using either VGA or HDMI ports to the projector. Note that mac laptops do not have VGA or HDMI ports and will need an adapter to connect to the projector.

  • Turn the AV system on and press the icon for Laptop VGA or Laptop HDMI for the connection you will be using.
  • Connect either the VGA or HDMI cable from the lectern to the laptop. If you are using an adapter connect the cable to the adapter first and then connect it to the laptop
  • If you are using the VGA connection and will need audio connect the audio plug from the lectern cable into the headphone jack on the laptop.
  • If the laptop does not automatically display through the projector you will need to configure it to use an external display
  • Internt Access - You can either use USFWireless or there is a network cable in the lectern that can connect to the laptop.

Classroom built in computer

The lectern in each classroom has a built in monitor and keyboard that connect to a mac mini computer that has both Mac and Windows 7 operating systems on it. To user the computer turn the AV system on and press the icon labeled Mac Mini.

  • To access the monitor and keyboard lift up the lectern cover and fold it back. Pull the top of the monitor screen up to tilt it so you can see it. The keyboard and trackpad are located in a drawer on the bottom of the lectern.
  • The monitor has a green light on the bottom edge of it when it is turned on. If the light is not on press the power button located on the bottom edge of the monitor.
  • If the screen is blank or has a 'no signal' message you will need to press the mac mini power button which is located in the AV rack. For fourth floor rooms the AV rack is located in a cabinet in the back of the room. For 5th floor classrooms it is located in the cabinet underneath the chalkboard closest to the door The power button has a yellow label next to it
  • Choosing mac or windows - When the computer starts up it displays a menu where you can choose to use either Mac or Windows. If at anytime you need to switch between Mac or Windows restart the computer to bring up this menu.
  • Connecting a USB device There is a USB connection to the built in computer on the lectern to the right of the monitor. If you need to connect more than one device there are two more USB connections in the AV rack

Group Study Rooms

Bloomberg Computers

Each group study room has a computer with Bloomberg software located in the cabinet below the display. To use the computer:

  • Turn the computer on if it is not already on
  • Check the power status light on the lower right corner of the display. If it is orange press the power button on the bottom of the display.
  • If the computer is not displaying on the display press the button on the source button on the bottom of the display repeatedly until it switches to BNC

Connecting a laptop to the display

Laptops can be connected to the groups study rooms displays using a VGA connection. Mac laptops will need a VGA adapter.

  • Check the power status light on the lower right corner of the display. If it is orange press the power button on the bottom of the display.
  • Press the source button on the bottom of the display repeatedly until it switches to PC
  • Connect the VGA connection cable in the cetner table console to the laptop
  • If the laptop does not automatically display through the projector you will need to configure it to use an external display
